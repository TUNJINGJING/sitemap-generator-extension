Privacy Policy for Sitemap Generator Chrome Extension

Last updated: February 2024

Data Collection and Usage

This extension only collects website content data necessary for generating sitemaps. We do not collect any personal information, authentication data, or browsing history.

Data Storage

All data processing occurs locally in your browser. We do not store or transmit any data to external servers.

Third-Party Access

We do not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties.

Data Security

All operations are performed locally within your browser, ensuring data security and privacy.

Updates

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. Please check back for any changes.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please use the feedback form within the extension.
